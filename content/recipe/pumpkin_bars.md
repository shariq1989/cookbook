Servings: 4-6<!--more-->

#### INGREDIENTS 

* 1 CUP Oil 
* 4 Eggs 
* 2 CUPS of Pumpkin Puree 
* 2 CUPS of Sugar 
* 2 CUPS of flour 
* 2 TSP. Baking Powder 
* 1 TSP. Baking Soda 
* ½ TSP. Salt 
* ¼ TSP. Ground Cloves 
* 2 TSP. Ground Cinnamon 

For the Frosting: 

* 3 OZ. Cream Cheese 
* ¼ CUP Butter 
* ½ TSP. Vanilla Extract 
* 1 TSP. Milk 
* Sprinkle with powdered sugar 

#### DIRECTIONS 

1. Beat and pour into a greased jelly roll. 
2. Bake for 350 degrees for 25 min.
