Servings: 4 <!--more-->

#### INGREDIENTS 

* ¾ cup All-Purpose Flour 
* ¼ cup Cornstarch 
* ½ tsp Baking Powder 
* ½ tsp Salt 
* 1 cup Milk 
* ½ cup Vegetable Oil 
* 1 Egg 
* 1½ tsp Sugar 
* ¾ tsp Vanilla Extract 

#### DIRECTIONS 
1. Mix ingredients well. Let stand for 30 minutes. 
2. Preheat the waffle iron. 
3. Add ¼ cup mix to the waffle iron.
