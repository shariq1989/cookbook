Servings: 4 <!--more-->

#### INGREDIENTS 

* ¾ CUP Flour 
* ¼ CUP Cornstarch 
* ½ TSP. Baking Powder 
* ½ TSP. Salt 
* 1 CUP Milk 
* ½ CUP Oil 
* 1 Egg 
* 1 ½ TSP. Sugar 
* ¾ TSP. Vanilla Extract 

#### DIRECTIONS 
1. Mix ingredients well. Let stand for 30 min. 
2. Preheat the waffle iron. 
3. Add ¼ CUP mix to the waffle iron.
