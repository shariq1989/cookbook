Servings: 4 <!--more-->

#### INGREDIENTS 

* ¾ Cup Flour 
* ¼ Cup Cornstarch 
* ½ Tsp. Baking Powder 
* ½ Tsp. Salt 
* 1 Cup Milk 
* ½ Cup Vegetable Oil 
* 1 Egg 
* 1 ½ Tsp. Sugar 
* ¾ Tsp. Vanilla Extract 

#### DIRECTIONS 
1. Mix ingredients well. Let stand for 30 min. 
2. Preheat the waffle iron. 
3. Add ¼ Cup mix to the waffle iron.
