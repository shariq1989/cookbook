Servings: 4 <!--more-->

#### INGREDIENTS 
    ¾ CUP Flour 
    ¼ CUP Cornstarch 
    ½ TSP. Baking Powder 
    ½ TSP. Salt 
    1 CUP Milk 
    ½ CUP Oil 
    1 Egg 
    1 ½ TSP. Sugar 
    ¾ TSP. Vanilla Extract 

#### DIRECTIONS 
    Mix ingredients well. Let stand for 30 min. 
    Preheat the waffle iron. 
    Add ¼ CUP mix to the waffle iron.
