### How to Keema Masala

#### Ingredients 

* 4 Black Cloves 
* 1 Cinnamon Stick, broken up
* 4 Green Cardamom
* 1 tsp Black Peppercorns
* 2 tsp Cumin Seed 
* 1/2 tsp Turmeric 
* 2 tsp Kashmiri Chili Powder
* 2 tsp Coriander Powder
* 1 tsp Salt
